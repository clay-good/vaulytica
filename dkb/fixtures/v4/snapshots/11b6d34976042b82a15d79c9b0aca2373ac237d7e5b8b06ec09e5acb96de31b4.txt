755 ILCS 5 — Probate Act of 1975.

§ 4-3 Signing and attestation. (a) Every will shall be in writing, signed by the testator or by some person in his presence and by his direction and attested in the presence of the testator by 2 or more credible witnesses. (b) A will that qualifies as an international will under the Uniform International Wills Act is considered to meet the requirements of subsection (a).
