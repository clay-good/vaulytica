Delaware General Corporation Law, Title 8 of the Delaware Code.

§ 102 Certificate of Incorporation. The certificate of incorporation shall state the name of the corporation, the address of its registered office in the State of Delaware, the nature of the business, the number of shares of authorized stock, and the name and mailing address of the incorporator.

§ 109 Bylaws. Bylaws may be adopted, amended, or repealed by stockholders entitled to vote. The original or other bylaws of a corporation may be adopted by the incorporators.

§ 141 Board of Directors. The business and affairs of every corporation organized under this chapter shall be managed by or under the direction of a board of directors.

§ 211 Meetings of Stockholders. An annual meeting of stockholders shall be held for the election of directors on a date and at a time designated by or in the manner provided in the bylaws.

§ 251 Merger or Consolidation of Domestic Corporations. The board of directors of each corporation that desires to merge or consolidate shall adopt an agreement of merger or consolidation. A plan of merger is required.

§ 262 Appraisal Rights. Any stockholder of a corporation of this State who holds shares of stock on the date of the making of a demand pursuant to subsection (d) of this section shall be entitled to appraisal rights.
