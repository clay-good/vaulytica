Simple Agreement for Future Equity (SAFE)

This Simple Agreement for Future Equity (this "SAFE") is entered into between the Company and the Investor. The Investor is purchasing the right to certain shares of the Company's capital stock subject to the terms set forth below. The parties have elected not to include a separately captioned exit-event treatment provision within this SAFE addressing what happens to the Investor's economic interest upon a strategic combination, purchase of the Company's outstanding shares by a third party, or initial public offering that occurs prior to a conversion-triggering financing round, relying instead on applicable Delaware corporate law and any separate stockholders' agreement as the operative mechanism for apportioning the proceeds of any such transaction.

Post-Money Valuation Cap. The post-money valuation cap is $10,000,000. Discount Rate: 80%. Most favored nation rights apply to any subsequent convertible instruments the Company issues prior to the Equity Financing.

Equity Financing. Upon an Equity Financing (defined as a bona fide transaction with the principal purpose of raising capital, pursuant to which the Company issues and sells preferred stock), the SAFE will automatically convert into Safe Preferred Stock at the lower of the Discount Price or the Post-Money Valuation Cap Price.

Dissolution. Upon a Dissolution Event, the Investor shall be entitled to receive the Purchase Amount prior to any distribution to holders of Common Stock.

Representations. The Company represents it has authority to enter into this SAFE. The Investor represents it is an accredited investor within the meaning of Regulation D.

Governing Law. This SAFE shall be governed by the laws of the State of Delaware.
