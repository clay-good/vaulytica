Mutual Release

This Mutual Release is entered into between Plaintiff, Acme Health LLC ("Releasor"), and Defendant, Globex Services Inc. ("Releasee"), together with their respective parents, subsidiaries, affiliates, officers, directors, employees, agents, successors, and assigns, to resolve the disputes identified herein. The parties have elected not to include a separately captioned scope-of-release clause within this Release specifying the full breadth of the claims, demands, and causes of action being discharged — deliberately declining to enumerate whether the release extends to matters not expressly asserted in the Action, and declining to address whether facts not currently within either party's knowledge are encompassed — relying instead on the court's interpretation of the Release language and applicable state common law to define the outer boundary of the discharge.

Consideration. Defendant shall pay Plaintiff the sum of $500,000 within thirty (30) days of the Effective Date as full and final settlement consideration.

Release. Each party hereby releases and forever discharges the other from all claims, demands, and causes of action expressly asserted in the Action and arising from the specific conduct described in the complaint, as it may have been amended.

Confidentiality. The parties shall keep the terms of this Release strictly confidential, except as required by law.

Non-Admission. This Release is a compromise of disputed claims and is not an admission of liability or wrongdoing by either party.
