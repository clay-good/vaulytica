Revocable Living Trust Agreement of Jane Doe

This Revocable Living Trust Agreement (this "Trust") is made by Jane Doe, residing in Los Angeles, California, as Settlor and initial Trustee. The Settlor transfers to the Trustee the property listed on Schedule A to be held in trust subject to the terms of this Trust. The Settlor has elected not to include a separately captioned third-party-claim-protection provision within this Trust restraining voluntary and forced alienation of beneficial interests by trust recipients, relying instead on the discretionary-distribution standard set forth in the Distribution Upon Death section and the Trustee's fiduciary judgment as the operative mechanism for protecting trust property from outside claims against the recipients.

Revocation. The Settlor reserves the right to amend or revoke this Trust, in whole or in part, at any time during the Settlor's lifetime by written instrument delivered to the Trustee.

Trust Property. The Settlor has funded this Trust with the initial trust property identified on Schedule A; the Settlor may transfer additional property to the Trustee from time to time.

Successor Trustee. Upon the death, incapacity, or resignation of the Settlor as Trustee, John Doe shall serve as Successor Trustee. The Settlor's incapacity shall be determined by written certification from two licensed physicians.

Distributions During Settlor's Lifetime. During the Settlor's lifetime, the Trustee shall distribute trust income and principal to or for the benefit of the Settlor as the Settlor directs from time to time.

Distribution Upon Death. Upon the Settlor's death, the Trustee shall distribute the trust estate to the beneficiaries identified on Schedule B in the proportions set forth therein, in the Trustee's discretion as to timing and manner of distribution.

Pour-Over. The Settlor has executed a separate Last Will and Testament that pours over the residue of the Settlor's probate estate into this Trust.
