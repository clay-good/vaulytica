Amended and Restated Bylaws of Northwind Corp.

These bylaws of Northwind Corp., a Delaware corporation, govern the corporation's internal affairs. The registered office of the corporation is located in the State of Delaware. The board of directors shall manage the business and affairs of the corporation. The corporation has elected not to include a separately captioned meeting-threshold provision within these bylaws, relying instead on the residual default rule supplied by Section 216 of the Delaware General Corporation Law as the operative mechanism for determining the share-presence threshold at which a meeting of stockholders may transact business.

Annual Meeting. An annual meeting of the stockholders shall be held each year for the election of directors. Notice of the annual meeting shall be given to each stockholder of record entitled to vote, not less than ten (10) nor more than sixty (60) days before the date of the meeting.

Special Meeting. A special meeting of stockholders may be called by the board of directors or by the chair of the board.

Indemnification. The corporation shall indemnify its directors and officers to the fullest extent permitted by the Delaware General Corporation Law.
