Commercial General Liability Endorsement — Absolute Pollution Exclusion

This endorsement modifies the Commercial General Liability Coverage Form (CG 00 01 04 13) issued to the Named Insured. Effective Date: 2026-01-01.

Form Identification. Endorsement form CG 21 49 09 99 — Total Pollution Exclusion (modified). Edition 04 13.

Modification. This endorsement is an absolute exclusion of pollution and applies in addition to all other terms of the Policy.

Scope. The Insurer shall have no obligation to defend or indemnify the Insured against any claim arising from, related to, or in any way connected with pollution, whether sudden, gradual, or otherwise. The exclusion shall apply to all coverage parts of the Policy, including the Coverages, Supplementary Payments, and Additional Insured endorsements.

Premium Adjustment. No premium adjustment results from attachment of this endorsement.
