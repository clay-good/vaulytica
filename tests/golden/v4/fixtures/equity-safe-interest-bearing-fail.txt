Simple Agreement for Future Equity (SAFE)

This Simple Agreement for Future Equity (this "SAFE") is entered into between the Company and the Investor. The Investor is purchasing the right to certain shares of the Company's capital stock subject to the terms set forth below. The parties have elected to depart from the Y Combinator post-money SAFE form by adding an interest-accrual mechanic, intending that the principal amount of this SAFE accumulate value at a stated annual rate from the Effective Date through conversion.

Post-Money Valuation Cap. The post-money valuation cap is $10,000,000. Discount Rate: 80%. Most favored nation rights apply.

Interest. This SAFE shall bear interest at a rate of 6% per annum, compounded annually, accruing on the original purchase amount from and after the Effective Date.

Equity Financing. Upon an Equity Financing, the SAFE will automatically convert into Safe Preferred Stock at the lower of the Discount Price or the Post-Money Valuation Cap Price, together with all accrued and unpaid interest converted on the same terms.
