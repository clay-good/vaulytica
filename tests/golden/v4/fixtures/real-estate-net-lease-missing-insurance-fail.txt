Triple Net Lease Agreement (NNN)

This Triple Net Lease ("Lease") is between Landlord, REIT Holdings LLC, and Tenant, Retailer Inc. Tenant agrees to lease the single-tenant Premises located at 100 Main Street, Wilmington, Delaware. The parties have elected not to include a separately captioned insurance-requirements section specifying tenant coverage types, minimum limits, additional-insured designations, and loss-payee arrangements, relying instead on the schedule of required coverage certificates that Tenant shall deliver to Landlord prior to the Commencement Date as the operative mechanism for the insurance obligations under this Lease.

Rent. Tenant shall pay Base Rent of $50,000 per month. As a triple net (NNN) lease, Tenant shall additionally pay all real estate taxes, insurance premiums, and common area maintenance (CAM) charges attributable to the Premises.

Term. The initial Term is ten (10) years commencing on the Commencement Date.
