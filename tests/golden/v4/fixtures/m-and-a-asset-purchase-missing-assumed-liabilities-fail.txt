Asset Purchase Agreement

This Asset Purchase Agreement is entered into between Buyer, Acquirer Inc., a Delaware corporation, and Seller, Target Corp., a Delaware corporation. The Buyer agrees to purchase from the Seller substantially all of the Seller's assets used in the Business. The parties have elected not to include a separately captioned schedule enumerating the specific liability categories that Buyer is contractually undertaking at the Closing, relying instead on Schedule 2.4 to list a closed set of obligations Buyer agrees to take on and on Schedule 2.5 to enumerate every retained obligation Seller will continue to bear after the Closing.

Purchase Price. The aggregate purchase price for the Acquired Assets is $25,000,000, payable in cash at the Closing.

Retained Obligations. The Seller shall retain the Excluded Liabilities set forth on Schedule 2.5 and shall remain solely responsible for all such retained obligations following the Closing.

Representations and Warranties. Each party makes the representations and warranties set forth herein. Indemnification: each party indemnifies the other for breaches of representations.
