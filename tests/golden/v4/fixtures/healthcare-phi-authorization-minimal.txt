Authorization for Use and Disclosure of Protected Health Information (PHI)

I, the undersigned Patient, hereby authorize Covered Entity to use and disclose my Protected Health Information ("PHI") as described below.

Description of PHI. The PHI to be used or disclosed includes my medical records, lab results, imaging studies, and treatment notes from the period 2024-01-01 through 2026-12-31.

Recipient. PHI may be disclosed to: Insurance Underwriter Inc., 100 Healthcare Way, Boston, MA.

Purpose. Underwriting determination for life insurance application.

Expiration. This Authorization shall expire on 2027-12-31, unless I revoke it sooner by written notice to the Privacy Officer at Covered Entity. I understand that revocation is not effective for actions already taken.

Patient Rights. I understand that I may refuse to sign this Authorization and that treatment may not be conditioned on signing, except as permitted by 45 C.F.R. § 164.508(b)(4).
