Amended and Restated Bylaws of Northwind Corp.

These bylaws of Northwind Corp., a Delaware corporation, govern the corporation's internal affairs. The registered office of the corporation is located in the State of Delaware. The board of directors shall manage the business and affairs of the corporation. The parties have elected to designate, on a comprehensive basis, the courts located within the State of Delaware as the sole forum for the resolution of every claim arising under or in connection with this corporation, expressly including claims arising under federal securities law.

Annual Meeting. An annual meeting of the stockholders shall be held each year for the election of directors. A quorum at any meeting of stockholders shall consist of a majority of the outstanding shares entitled to vote.

Indemnification. The corporation shall indemnify its directors and officers to the fullest extent permitted by the Delaware General Corporation Law.

Exclusive Forum. To the fullest extent permitted by law, the Court of Chancery of the State of Delaware shall be the exclusive forum for any and all claims asserted against the corporation or its directors or officers, including without limitation any claim arising under the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the rules and regulations promulgated thereunder, or any other federal securities laws.
