Authorization for Use and Disclosure of Protected Health Information (PHI)

I, the undersigned Patient, hereby authorize Covered Entity to use and disclose my Protected Health Information ("PHI") as described below. The parties have elected not to include a separately captioned conditioning-prohibition provision within this Authorization stating that signing is not a prerequisite to receiving care, services, or coverage, relying instead on the Covered Entity's internal policies and the applicable federal privacy regulations as the operative source for any such assurance.

Description of PHI. The PHI to be used or disclosed includes my medical records, lab results, imaging studies, and clinical notes from the period 2024-01-01 through 2026-12-31.

Recipient. PHI may be disclosed to: Insurance Underwriter Inc., 100 Healthcare Way, Boston, MA.

Purpose. Underwriting determination for life insurance application.

Expiration. This Authorization shall expire on 2027-12-31, unless I revoke it sooner by written notice to the Privacy Officer at Covered Entity. I understand that revocation is not effective for actions already taken.

Patient Rights. I understand that I may decline to sign this Authorization. Signing is entirely voluntary under 45 C.F.R. § 164.508(b)(4).
