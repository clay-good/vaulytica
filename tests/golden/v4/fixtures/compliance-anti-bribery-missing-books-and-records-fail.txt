Anti-Bribery and Anti-Corruption Policy

This Anti-Bribery and Anti-Corruption Policy applies to all directors, officers, employees, agents, and third parties acting on behalf of Acme Global Inc. and its subsidiaries. The parties have elected not to include a separately captioned financial-records-and-controls provision within this Policy addressing the federal accounting-provisions regime that mandates complete and faithful ledger keeping or the supporting governance framework for transaction integrity, relying instead on the Chief Financial Officer's quarterly attestation and the separately maintained Global Finance Manual as the operative source for any such recordation and control obligation.

Prohibition. Acme strictly prohibits any form of bribery or corruption, including the giving or receiving of anything of value, directly or indirectly, to or from any government official or commercial counterparty for the purpose of improperly obtaining or retaining business or any improper advantage.

Compliance with Law. This Policy is intended to comply with the U.S. Foreign Corrupt Practices Act of 1977 ("FCPA"), the U.K. Bribery Act 2010, and all other applicable anti-corruption laws in the jurisdictions where Acme operates.

Reporting. Any actual or suspected violation must be reported to the Compliance Officer. Acme prohibits retaliation against any employee who reports a suspected violation in good faith.

Training and Certification. Employees in high-risk roles shall complete annual anti-bribery training and certify compliance.
