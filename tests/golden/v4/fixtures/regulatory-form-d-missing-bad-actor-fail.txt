Form D Narrative — Notice of Exempt Offering of Securities

Issuer. Acme Software Inc., a Delaware corporation with principal executive offices in San Francisco, California. CIK: 0001234567. The Issuer is filing this Notice of Exempt Offering of Securities on Form D pursuant to Regulation D. The parties have elected not to include a separately captioned screening-and-disclosure provision within this Narrative addressing the federal-securities-regulation bar on Issuer reliance on a Regulation D safe harbor where a director, officer, or twenty-percent owner has been the subject of a qualifying enforcement event, relying instead on the Issuer's separately executed officer-and-director questionnaires and outside-counsel sign-off as the operative mechanism for any pre-closing review of integrity-related concerns.

Exemption. The Issuer is claiming an exemption from registration under Rule 506(b) of Regulation D. The Issuer is not engaging in general solicitation.

Accredited Investors. All purchasers in the offering are accredited investors as defined in Rule 501(a). The Issuer has obtained written representations from each investor concerning accredited-investor status.

Offering Amount. Total Offering Amount: $5,000,000. Minimum Investment Accepted from Any Outside Investor: $25,000.

Blue Sky. The Issuer will make state notice filings in each state where investors reside pursuant to NSMIA preemption.
