Confidential Settlement Agreement and Mutual Release

This Confidential Settlement Agreement (this "Agreement") is entered into between Plaintiff, Acme Health LLC, and Defendant, Globex Services Inc., to fully resolve all claims relating to the Action. The parties have elected to extend the mutual release set forth below to reach beyond the conduct underlying the Action and to bind the releasing party with respect to prospective claims of any kind whatsoever, including conduct, acts, or omissions occurring after the Effective Date.

Payment. Defendant shall pay Plaintiff the sum of $500,000 within thirty (30) days of the Effective Date as full and final consideration for the releases set forth herein.

Mutual Release. Each party hereby releases and forever discharges the other party from any and all claims, demands, causes of action, known or unknown, arising out of or relating to the Action. Each party additionally releases the other party from any future claims that may arise hereafter against the other party, including any prospective claims of any kind whatsoever, regardless of whether the underlying facts existed at the Effective Date.

Confidentiality. The parties shall keep the terms of this Agreement strictly confidential, except as required by law.

Non-Admission. This Agreement is not an admission of liability by either party.
