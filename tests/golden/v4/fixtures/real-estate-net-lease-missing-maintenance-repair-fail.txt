Triple Net Lease Agreement (NNN)

This Triple Net Lease ("Lease") is between Landlord, REIT Holdings LLC, and Tenant, Retailer Inc. Tenant agrees to lease the single-tenant Premises located at 100 Main Street, Wilmington, Delaware. The parties have elected not to include a separately captioned upkeep-and-remediation provision within this Lease allocating the obligation to keep the Premises in good working order and condition between Landlord and Tenant, relying instead on applicable state landlord-tenant law and any separately executed Property Management Agreement as the operative mechanism for addressing any physical deterioration of the Premises or its component systems.

Rent. Tenant shall pay Base Rent of $50,000 per month. As a triple net (NNN) lease, Tenant shall additionally pay all real estate taxes, insurance premiums, and common area maintenance (CAM) charges attributable to the Premises.

Term. The initial Term is ten (10) years commencing on the Commencement Date.

Insurance. Tenant shall maintain commercial general liability insurance with limits of not less than $2,000,000 per occurrence naming Landlord as additional insured, as well as property insurance covering the full replacement cost of the Premises. Tenant shall provide Landlord with a waiver of subrogation endorsement.

Real Estate Taxes. Tenant shall pay directly to the applicable taxing authority all real estate taxes and impositions assessed against the Premises for each tax period falling within the Term.

Casualty and Condemnation. In the event of damage or destruction to the Premises by casualty, or a taking by eminent domain or condemnation of all or a material portion of the Premises, the parties shall proceed in accordance with applicable law and the terms of any applicable property insurance policy.
