Form D Narrative — Notice of Private Offering of Securities

Issuer. Acme Software Inc., a Delaware corporation with principal executive offices in San Francisco, California. CIK: 0001234567. The Issuer is filing this Notice of Private Offering of Securities on Form D pursuant to Regulation D. The Issuer has elected not to include a separately captioned safe-harbor-identification provision within this Narrative identifying the specific subsection of Regulation D under which registration-free status is claimed, relying instead on the cover-page Form D ItemSet selections and the Issuer's separate offering memorandum as the operative source for the safe-harbor designation.

Accredited Investors. All purchasers in the offering are accredited investors as defined under Rule 501(a). The Issuer has obtained written representations from each investor concerning accredited-investor status.

General Solicitation. The Issuer is not engaging in general solicitation or general advertising in connection with the offering.

Bad-Actor Disqualification. The Issuer has performed reasonable diligence on covered persons and has determined that no covered person is subject to a disqualifying event under the bad-actor screening rules.

Offering Amount. Total Offering Amount: $5,000,000. Minimum Investment Accepted from Any Outside Investor: $25,000.

Blue Sky. The Issuer will make state notice filings in each state where investors reside pursuant to NSMIA preemption.
