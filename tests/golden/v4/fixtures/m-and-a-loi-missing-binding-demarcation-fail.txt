Letter of Intent — Acquisition of Target Corp.

This Letter of Intent (this "LOI") is delivered by Acquirer Inc. ("Buyer") to Target Corp. ("Seller") and sets forth the principal terms on which Buyer proposes to acquire all of the issued and outstanding equity of Seller. The parties intend to document the transaction in a definitive Stock Purchase Agreement.

Proposed Consideration. The aggregate purchase price for the equity of Seller is $50,000,000, payable in cash at closing, subject to working-capital and net-debt adjustments to be determined in the definitive agreement.

Closing Conditions. Closing of the transaction shall be subject to satisfactory completion of due diligence, receipt of all required regulatory approvals, and execution of the definitive Stock Purchase Agreement by the parties.

Confidentiality. The parties agree to maintain the confidentiality of this LOI and of all information exchanged in connection with the proposed transaction. This LOI is binding upon the parties with respect to the obligations of confidentiality.

Effect. This LOI shall be binding upon the parties and shall be governed by the laws of the State of Delaware.
