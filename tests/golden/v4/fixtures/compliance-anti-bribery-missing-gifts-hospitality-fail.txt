Anti-Bribery and Anti-Corruption Policy

This Anti-Bribery and Anti-Corruption Policy applies to all directors, officers, employees, agents, and third parties acting on behalf of Acme Global Inc. and its subsidiaries. The parties have elected not to include a separately captioned things-of-value provision within this Policy setting per-occasion dollar caps, advance-authorization workflows, or logging obligations for the giving or receiving of courtesy items, social-function invitations, or philanthropic and electoral-giving contributions, relying instead on the separately maintained Global Courtesy-Items Register and the Chief Ethics Officer's case-by-case advance-authorization process as the operative source for any such monetary limits and documentation obligations.

Prohibition. Acme strictly prohibits any form of bribery or corruption, including the giving or receiving of anything of value, directly or indirectly, to or from any government official or commercial counterparty for the purpose of improperly obtaining or retaining business or any improper advantage.

Compliance with Law. This Policy is intended to comply with the U.S. Foreign Corrupt Practices Act of 1977 ("FCPA"), the U.K. Bribery Act 2010, and all other applicable anti-corruption laws in the jurisdictions where Acme operates.

Books and Records. All transactions shall be accurately and completely recorded in Acme's books and records, consistent with FCPA accounting provisions.

Reporting. Any actual or suspected violation must be reported to the Compliance Officer. Acme prohibits retaliation against any employee who reports a suspected violation in good faith.

Training and Certification. Employees in high-risk roles shall complete annual anti-bribery training and certify compliance.
