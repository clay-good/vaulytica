Revocable Living Trust Agreement of Jane Doe

This Revocable Living Trust Agreement (this "Trust") is made by Jane Doe, residing in Los Angeles, California, as Settlor and initial Trustee. The Settlor hereby transfers to the Trustee the property listed on Schedule A to be held in trust subject to the terms of this Trust. The Settlor has elected not to include a separately captioned provision within this Trust addressing how the Trustee shall deal with trust assets for the Settlor's benefit so long as the Settlor remains alive, relying instead on the Trustee's general fiduciary duties and the Settlor's retained power to direct the Trustee at any time as the operative mechanism for managing trust assets on the Settlor's behalf.

Revocation. The Settlor reserves the right to amend or revoke this Trust, in whole or in part, at any time by written instrument delivered to the Trustee.

Trust Property. The Settlor has funded this Trust with the initial trust property identified on Schedule A; the Settlor may transfer additional property to the Trustee from time to time.

Successor Trustee. Upon the death, incapacity, or resignation of the Settlor as Trustee, John Doe shall serve as Successor Trustee. The Settlor's incapacity shall be determined by written certification from two licensed physicians.

Death Disposition. Upon the Settlor's death, the Trustee shall pay out the trust estate to the beneficiaries identified on Schedule B in the proportions set forth therein.

Pour-Over. The Settlor has executed a separate Last Will and Testament that pours over the residue of the Settlor's probate estate into this Trust.

Spendthrift. No beneficiary may voluntarily or involuntarily transfer, assign, anticipate, or encumber any beneficial interest in this Trust. No creditor of any beneficiary or any attachment may reach any beneficial interest before actual payment to such beneficiary.
