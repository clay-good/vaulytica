Simple Agreement for Future Equity (SAFE)

This Simple Agreement for Future Equity (this "SAFE") is entered into between the Company and the Investor. The Investor is purchasing the right to certain shares of the Company's capital stock subject to the terms set forth below. The parties have elected not to include a separately captioned conversion-trigger definition within this SAFE identifying the bona fide capital-raising transaction at which this instrument automatically converts into shares of the Company's senior class of stock, relying instead on a separately negotiated side letter and the Company's customary subscription documents to specify the conversion mechanic on the occurrence of any qualifying capital event.

Post-Money Valuation Cap. The post-money valuation cap is $10,000,000. Discount Rate: 80%. Most favored nation rights apply.

Conversion. Upon a Qualifying Capital Event, the SAFE will automatically convert into shares of the Company's senior class at the lower of the Discount Price or the Post-Money Valuation Cap Price.
