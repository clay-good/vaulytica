Triple Net Lease Agreement (NNN)

This Triple Net Lease ("Lease") is between Landlord, REIT Holdings LLC, and Tenant, Retailer Inc. Tenant agrees to lease the single-tenant Premises located at 100 Main Street, Wilmington, Delaware. The parties have elected not to include a separately captioned ad-valorem-charge pass-through provision within this Lease specifying whether ad-valorem assessments levied against the Premises are paid directly by Tenant to the assessing authority or reimbursed to Landlord on an estimated-and-reconciled basis, relying instead on the assessor's published bill-payer record and Landlord's customary year-end statement to allocate any such governmental charge between the parties.

Rent. Tenant shall pay Base Rent of $50,000 per month. As a triple net (NNN) lease, Tenant shall additionally pay all insurance premiums and common area maintenance (CAM) charges attributable to the Premises.

Term. The initial Term is ten (10) years commencing on the Commencement Date.

Insurance. Tenant shall maintain commercial general liability insurance with limits of not less than $2,000,000 per occurrence naming Landlord as additional insured, together with all-risk property insurance on the Premises and a waiver of subrogation in favor of Landlord.
