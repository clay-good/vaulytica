Confidential Settlement Agreement and Mutual Release

This Confidential Settlement Agreement (this "Agreement") is entered into between Plaintiff, Acme Health LLC, and Defendant, Globex Services Inc., to fully resolve all claims relating to the Action. The parties have elected not to include a separately captioned compensation clause stating the monetary amount to be paid for the releases set forth herein, relying instead on the delivery of certain non-monetary undertakings documented in a separately executed side letter as the operative mechanism for resolving the financial aspects of this matter.

Mutual Release. Each party hereby releases and forever discharges the other party from any and all claims, demands, causes of action, known or unknown, arising out of or relating to the Action.

Confidentiality. The parties shall keep the terms of this Agreement strictly confidential, except as required by law.

Non-Admission. This Agreement is not an admission of liability by either party.
