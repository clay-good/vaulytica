Revocable Living Trust Agreement of Jane Doe

This Revocable Living Trust Agreement (this "Trust") is made by Jane Doe, residing in Los Angeles, California, as Settlor and initial Trustee. The Settlor hereby transfers to the Trustee the property listed on Schedule A to be held in trust subject to the terms of this Trust. The parties have elected not to include a separately captioned probate-coordination provision within this Trust referencing the Settlor's residuary-disposition instrument or otherwise coordinating the post-death funding of this Trust from the Settlor's separately administered probate estate, relying instead on the Settlor's attorney's file and the personal representative's good-faith administration as the operative source for any such residuary-funding step.

Revocation. The Settlor reserves the right to amend or revoke this Trust, in whole or in part, at any time during the Settlor's lifetime by written instrument delivered to the Trustee.

Successor Trustee. Upon the death, incapacity, or resignation of the Settlor as Trustee, John Doe shall serve as Successor Trustee. The Settlor's incapacity shall be determined by written certification from two licensed physicians.

Distribution Upon Death. Upon the Settlor's death, the Trustee shall distribute the trust estate to the beneficiaries identified on Schedule B in the proportions set forth therein.
