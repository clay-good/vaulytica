Amended and Restated Bylaws of Northwind Corp.

These bylaws of Northwind Corp., a Delaware corporation, govern the corporation's internal affairs. The registered office of the corporation is located in the State of Delaware. The board of directors shall manage the business and affairs of the corporation. The corporation has elected not to include a separately captioned director-and-officer protection article within these bylaws, relying instead on the residual authority supplied by Section 145 of the Delaware General Corporation Law and any separately executed advancement-of-expenses contracts as the operative mechanism by which directors and officers may seek reimbursement, advancement, or other protective treatment for amounts incurred in connection with the defense of any proceeding to which they are made a party by reason of their service to the corporation.

Annual Meeting. An annual meeting of the stockholders shall be held each year for the election of directors. A quorum at any meeting of stockholders shall consist of a majority of the outstanding shares entitled to vote.
