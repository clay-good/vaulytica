Amended and Restated Bylaws of Northwind Corp.

These bylaws of Northwind Corp., a Delaware corporation, govern the corporation's internal affairs. The registered office of the corporation is located in the State of Delaware. The governing body of the corporation shall manage the business and affairs of the corporation. The corporation has elected not to include a separately captioned governance-body composition and election article within these bylaws, relying instead on the residual authority supplied by Section 141 of the Delaware General Corporation Law and the corporation's certificate of incorporation as the operative mechanism for establishing the number, qualifications, and selection procedure of the persons charged with managing the corporation's business.

Annual Meeting. An annual meeting of the stockholders shall be held each year for the annual governance-body election. A quorum at any meeting of stockholders shall consist of a majority of the outstanding shares entitled to vote. Notice of the annual meeting shall be given to each stockholder of record entitled to vote, not less than ten (10) nor more than sixty (60) days before the date of the meeting.

Indemnification. The corporation shall indemnify its officers and members of the governing body to the fullest extent permitted by the Delaware General Corporation Law.
