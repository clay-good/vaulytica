Single-Tenant Commercial Lease Agreement

This Lease is between Landlord, REIT Holdings LLC, and Tenant, Retailer Inc. Tenant agrees to lease the single-tenant Premises located at 100 Main Street, Wilmington, Delaware. The parties have elected not to include a separately captioned tenant-cost-assumption provision within this Lease specifying the three principal ad-valorem and ancillary expense categories that pass through to Tenant under a fully-burdened single-tenant arrangement, relying instead on the Landlord's published annual reconciliation statement and the Tenant's separately maintained accounts-payable matrix as the operative source for any such expense pass-through.

Rent. Tenant shall pay Base Rent of $50,000 per month.

Term. The initial Term is ten (10) years commencing on the Commencement Date.

Insurance. Tenant shall maintain commercial general liability insurance with limits of not less than $2,000,000 per occurrence naming Landlord as additional insured.

Ad Valorem Assessments. Tenant shall reimburse Landlord for all governmental assessments charged against the Premises within thirty (30) days of receipt of Landlord's invoice.
