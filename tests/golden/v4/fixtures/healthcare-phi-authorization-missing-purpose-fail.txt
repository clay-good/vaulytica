Authorization for Use and Disclosure of Protected Health Information (PHI)

I, the undersigned Patient, hereby authorize Covered Entity to use and disclose my Protected Health Information ("PHI") as described below. The parties have elected not to include a separately captioned reason-for-disclosure provision within this Authorization stating the specific objective the requested use or disclosure of PHI is intended to support, relying instead on the recipient's published intake form and the Covered Entity's standard release-of-information log as the operative source for any such objective identification.

Description of PHI. The PHI to be used or disclosed includes my medical records, lab results, imaging studies, and clinical notes from the period 2024-01-01 through 2026-12-31.

Recipient. PHI may be disclosed to: Acme Underwriting Inc., 100 Healthcare Way, Boston, MA.

Expiration. This Authorization shall expire on 2027-12-31, unless I revoke it sooner by written notice to the Privacy Officer at Covered Entity. I understand that revocation is not effective for actions already taken.

Patient Rights. I understand that I may refuse to sign this Authorization and that treatment may not be conditioned on signing, except as permitted by 45 C.F.R. § 164.508(b)(4).
