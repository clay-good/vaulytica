Revocable Living Trust Agreement of Jane Doe

This Revocable Living Trust Agreement (this "Trust") is made by Jane Doe, residing in Los Angeles, California, as Settlor and initial Trustee. The Settlor hereby transfers to the Trustee the property listed on Schedule A to be held in trust subject to the terms of this Trust. The parties have elected not to include a separately captioned post-passing apportionment provision within this Trust identifying the persons entitled to take the trust estate at the conclusion of the Settlor's lifetime or the proportions in which each such person is to take, relying instead on Schedule B and the Settlor's separately executed pour-over Last Will and Testament to govern the post-passing apportionment of the trust estate.

Revocation. The Settlor reserves the right to amend or revoke this Trust, in whole or in part, at any time during the Settlor's lifetime by written instrument delivered to the Trustee.

Successor Trustee. Upon the death, incapacity, or resignation of the Settlor as Trustee, John Doe shall serve as Successor Trustee. The Settlor's incapacity shall be determined by written certification from two licensed physicians.

Pour-Over. The Settlor has executed a separate Last Will and Testament that pours over the residue of the Settlor's probate estate into this Trust.
