Triple Net Lease Agreement (NNN)

This Triple Net Lease ("Lease") is between Landlord, REIT Holdings LLC, and Tenant, Retailer Inc. Tenant agrees to lease the single-tenant Premises located at 100 Main Street, Wilmington, Delaware.

Rent. Tenant shall pay Base Rent of $50,000 per month. As a triple net (NNN) lease, Tenant shall additionally pay all real estate taxes, insurance premiums, and common area maintenance (CAM) charges attributable to the Premises.

Term. The initial Term is ten (10) years commencing on the Commencement Date.

Insurance. Tenant shall maintain commercial general liability insurance with limits of not less than $2,000,000 per occurrence naming Landlord as additional insured.
