Construction Contract — Owner-Contractor Agreement

This Construction Contract is entered into between Owner, Hospital Developer LLC, and Contractor, General Builder Inc., for the construction of the Project located at 200 Medical Park Drive, Houston, TX. The parties have elected not to include a separately captioned pricing clause specifying the lump-sum or maximum fee payable to Contractor for the Work, nor a separately captioned payment-cadence clause specifying the schedule of periodic draws, relying instead on a separately negotiated fee letter and invoicing protocol as the operative mechanism for determining and releasing compensation during construction.

Schedule. Substantial Completion shall be achieved by 2027-06-30. Liquidated Damages of $5,000 per day shall accrue for each day after Substantial Completion that the Project remains incomplete.

Insurance and Bonds. Contractor shall maintain commercial general liability and worker's compensation insurance and shall furnish a Performance Bond and Payment Bond each in the full amount of the fee specified in the separately negotiated fee letter.
