Commercial General Liability Policy Summary

This Policy Summary describes a commercial general liability policy issued by the Insurer to the Insured Party identified below. The parties have elected not to include a separately captioned party-and-intermediary identification provision within this Summary stating the full legal entity name of the coverage beneficiary or the producing wholesaler that procured the coverage, relying instead on the underlying binder, the original application package, and the carrier's internal book-of-business records as the operative source for those identifications.

Insured Party. Contractor LLC, 100 Builder Way, Austin, TX.

Insurance Intermediary. Insurance Wholesale Services LLC.

Policy Period. Inception 2026-01-01; expiration 2027-01-01 at 12:01 a.m. at the Insured Party's address.

Premium and Retention. The annual premium is $42,500. A per-occurrence deductible of $25,000 applies to each loss.

Coverage Trigger. The policy is written on an occurrence basis. No retroactive date or extended reporting period applies.

Limits of Liability. $2,000,000 each occurrence; $4,000,000 general aggregate; $4,000,000 products-completed operations aggregate.

Forms Schedule. CG 00 01 04 13 — Commercial General Liability Coverage Form (Occurrence). CG 20 10 04 13 — Additional Insured — Owners, Lessees or Contractors.

Additional Insured. Certificate Holder is named as additional insured on the General Liability policy as required by written contract.

Waiver of Subrogation. The Insurer waives rights of subrogation against the Certificate Holder where permitted by policy and applicable law.
