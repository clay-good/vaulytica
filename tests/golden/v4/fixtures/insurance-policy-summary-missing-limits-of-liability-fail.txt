Commercial General Liability Policy Summary

This Policy Summary describes a commercial general liability policy issued to the Named Insured by the Insurer through the listed Producer. The parties have elected not to include a separately captioned coverage-magnitude provision within this Summary specifying the per-event maximum payment obligation or the total maximum payment obligation borne by the Insurer under any coverage part, relying instead on the original binder, the declarations page, and any separately executed schedule of coverage caps as the operative source for the per-event and total maximum payment figures.

Named Insured. Contractor LLC, 100 Builder Way, Austin, TX.

Producer. Insurance Brokerage Services LLC.

Policy Period. Inception 2026-01-01; expiration 2027-01-01 at 12:01 a.m. at the Named Insured's address.

Premium and Retention. The annual premium is $42,500. A per-occurrence deductible of $25,000 applies to each loss.

Coverage Trigger. The policy is written on an occurrence basis. No retroactive date or extended reporting period applies.

Forms Schedule. CG 00 01 04 13 — Commercial General Liability Coverage Form (Occurrence). CG 20 10 04 13 — Additional Insured — Owners, Lessees or Contractors.

Additional Insured. Certificate Holder is named as additional insured on the General Liability policy as required by written contract.

Waiver of Subrogation. The Insurer waives rights of subrogation against the Certificate Holder where permitted by policy and applicable law.
