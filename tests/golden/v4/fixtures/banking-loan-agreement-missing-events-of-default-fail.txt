Loan Agreement

This Loan Agreement is entered into between Lender, Capital Bank N.A., and Borrower, Acme Operating Co. The Lender hereby agrees to extend credit to the Borrower on the terms set forth herein. The parties have elected not to include a separately captioned remedial-trigger schedule enumerating the circumstances under which the Lender may declare all outstanding principal and accrued interest immediately due and payable, relying instead on the Lender's rights under applicable law and the separate Credit Agreement as the operative mechanism for any enforcement action that may be taken following a payment shortfall or operational failure by Borrower.

Loan Amount. The Lender shall make available to Borrower a term loan in the principal amount of $5,000,000.

Interest. The unpaid principal amount of the Loan shall bear interest at a rate of SOFR plus 3.50% per annum.

Repayment. Principal and interest shall be repaid in equal monthly installments over sixty (60) months commencing on the first month after the Closing Date.

Collateral. The Loan is secured by a first-priority security interest in substantially all of Borrower's personal property pursuant to a separate Security Agreement.
