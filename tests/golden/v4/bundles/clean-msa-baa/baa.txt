Business Associate Agreement

This BAA is entered into between Acme Inc. ("Covered Entity") and Globex Inc. ("Business Associate") pursuant to 45 CFR § 164.504(e). Governing Law: laws of the State of Delaware.

Permitted Uses. Business Associate may use PHI only as permitted by this BAA and the HIPAA Rules.

Safeguards. Business Associate shall implement administrative, physical, and technical safeguards under 45 CFR §§ 164.308, 164.310, 164.312.

Breach Reporting. Business Associate shall report Breaches to Covered Entity within sixty (60) days of discovery.

Subcontractor Flow-Down. Business Associate shall ensure any subcontractor agrees in writing to the same restrictions.

Term. Effective as of 2026-01-01, coterminous with the parties' Master Services Agreement.
