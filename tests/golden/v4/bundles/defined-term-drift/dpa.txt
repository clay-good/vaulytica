Data Processing Addendum

This Data Processing Addendum ("Addendum") is entered into between Acme Inc. ("Controller") and Globex Inc. ("Processor"). Governing Law: laws of the State of Delaware.

Definitions. "Customer Data" means all personal data, sensitive personal data, and any derived analytics processed by Processor on behalf of Controller, including any data generated from Controller's end users.

Processing. Processor shall process Customer Data only on documented instructions from Controller pursuant to Article 28 of the GDPR. Customer Data shall not be used for any other purpose.

Subprocessors. Processor shall not engage any subprocessor without prior written consent of Controller and shall flow down equivalent obligations.

Security. Processor shall implement appropriate technical and organizational measures pursuant to Article 32 GDPR.

Data Retention. Processor shall delete or return all Customer Data upon termination of the Agreement.

Term. Effective as of 2026-01-01, coterminous with the parties' Master Services Agreement.
