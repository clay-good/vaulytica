Master Services Agreement

This Master Services Agreement ("Agreement") is between Acme Inc. ("Customer") and Globex Inc. ("Vendor"). Governing Law: laws of the State of Delaware.

Data Processing. To the extent Vendor processes personal data on behalf of Customer, the parties shall enter into a Data Processing Agreement as required by applicable law. The terms of any such data processing agreement shall be incorporated herein by reference.

Services. Vendor shall perform the Services described in each Statement of Work issued hereunder.

Limitation of Liability. Each party's aggregate liability under this Agreement shall not exceed one million dollars ($1,000,000).

Confidentiality. Each party shall maintain the confidentiality of the other party's Confidential Information.

Term. Initial Term: three (3) years commencing 2026-01-01.
