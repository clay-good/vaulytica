Business Associate Agreement

This Business Associate Agreement ("BAA") is entered into pursuant to 45 CFR § 164.504(e) between Acme Corp. ("Covered Entity") and Globex, Inc. ("Business Associate"). Business Associate creates or receives Protected Health Information ("PHI") on behalf of Covered Entity.

Permitted Uses. Business Associate may use and disclose PHI only as permitted by this BAA and the HIPAA Rules.

Safeguards. Business Associate shall implement appropriate administrative, physical, and technical safeguards consistent with 45 CFR §§ 164.308, 164.310, 164.312.

Reporting. Business Associate shall report Breaches without unreasonable delay and in no case later than sixty (60) days after discovery.

Subcontractor Flow-Down. Business Associate shall ensure subcontractors agree in writing to the same restrictions.
