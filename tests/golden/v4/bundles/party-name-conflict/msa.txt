Master Services Agreement

This Master Services Agreement is entered into between Acme Corporation, a Delaware corporation ("Customer"), and Globex Services Inc., a New York corporation ("Vendor"). The Vendor shall perform the Services described in each Statement of Work.

Governing Law. This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of New York.

Limitation of Liability. Each party's aggregate liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the fees paid in the twelve (12) months preceding the claim.

Confidentiality. Each party shall maintain the confidentiality of the other party's Confidential Information.

Term. The Initial Term is three (3) years commencing on 2026-01-01.
