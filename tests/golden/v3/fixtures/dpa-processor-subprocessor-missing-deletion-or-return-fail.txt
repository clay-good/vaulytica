Data Processing Agreement (Processor to Subprocessor)

This Sub-Processing Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of February 1, 2026, pursuant to Article 28(4) of the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679 ("GDPR"), between Globex EU SARL, a French société à responsabilité limitée acting as data processor ("Upstream Processor"), and Stark Cloud Ireland Ltd., an Irish private limited company acting as subprocessor ("Subprocessor"). The Upstream Processor acts on documented instructions received from one or more data controllers, and the Subprocessor will process personal data on behalf of the Upstream Processor in providing cloud infrastructure services under the Master Services Agreement between the parties dated January 1, 2026 (the "MSA").

1. Controller Authorization. The Upstream Processor has obtained prior specific written authorization from the relevant controller(s) to appoint the Subprocessor, in accordance with Article 28(2) GDPR. The controller is hereby named as a third-party beneficiary of this Agreement and may enforce the data-protection obligations hereunder directly against the Subprocessor to the same extent as the Upstream Processor.

2. Documented Instructions Only. The Subprocessor shall process personal data only on documented instructions from the Upstream Processor, which instructions reflect the upstream instructions provided by the relevant controller. The Subprocessor shall immediately inform the Upstream Processor if any instruction infringes the GDPR or applicable data protection law.

3. Same Obligations as Upstream DPA. The Subprocessor agrees to be bound by data-protection obligations equivalent to those imposed on the Upstream Processor by the controller under the controller-processor data processing agreement, including all obligations under Article 28 GDPR. A breach by the Subprocessor of its obligations under this Agreement shall be treated as a breach by the Upstream Processor of its obligations to the controller.

4. Confidentiality. The Subprocessor shall ensure that persons authorized to process personal data on its behalf are subject to appropriate confidentiality obligations, whether contractual or statutory.

5. Security Measures (Article 32 GDPR). The Subprocessor shall implement and maintain appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure a level of security appropriate to the risk, including: (a) encryption of personal data in transit and at rest; (b) pseudonymization where appropriate; (c) ongoing confidentiality, integrity, availability, and resilience of processing systems; (d) the ability to restore availability and access to personal data in a timely manner following an incident; (e) a process for regularly testing, assessing, and evaluating the effectiveness of technical and organizational security measures.

6. Assistance with Data Subject Rights and Controller Obligations. The Subprocessor shall assist the Upstream Processor, by appropriate technical and organizational measures, to enable the Upstream Processor to fulfill its obligations to assist the controller with: (a) data subject access requests and other rights under Chapter III GDPR; (b) security obligations under Article 32 GDPR; (c) personal data breach notification under Articles 33 and 34 GDPR; (d) data protection impact assessments under Article 35 GDPR; and (e) prior consultation with supervisory authorities under Article 36 GDPR.

7. Breach Notification. The Subprocessor shall notify the Upstream Processor without undue delay, and in any event within twenty-four (24) hours, after becoming aware of a personal data breach. Such notification shall include sufficient information for the Upstream Processor to notify the competent supervisory authority within seventy-two (72) hours of becoming aware, as required by Article 33 GDPR.

8. Sub-Subprocessors. The Subprocessor shall not engage any further subprocessor without prior specific or general written authorization of the Upstream Processor (which authorization is subject to obtaining the controller's prior written authorization where required). The Subprocessor shall impose equivalent data-protection obligations on any further subprocessor by written contract and shall remain liable to the Upstream Processor for the further subprocessor's performance.

9. Audit Rights. The Subprocessor shall make available to the Upstream Processor all information necessary to demonstrate compliance with this Agreement and shall allow for and contribute to audits, including inspections, conducted by the Upstream Processor or an auditor mandated by the Upstream Processor, subject to thirty (30) days prior written notice.

10. Cross-Border Transfer Mechanism. Personal data transferred from the Upstream Processor to the Subprocessor that involves a transfer outside the European Economic Area shall be governed by the Standard Contractual Clauses adopted by the European Commission pursuant to Commission Implementing Decision (EU) 2021/914, Module 3 (Processor to Processor), which are incorporated by reference and form part of this Agreement.

Annex I — Processing Description
Subject matter: cloud infrastructure and data processing services. Duration: term of the MSA. Nature: storage, retrieval, and processing. Purpose: fulfillment of services under the MSA. Categories of personal data: names, email addresses, IP addresses, usage logs. Data subjects: end-users of the controller's services.
