UK International Data Transfer Addendum to the EU Standard Contractual Clauses

This Addendum has been issued by the Information Commissioner for Parties making Restricted Transfers. The Information Commissioner considers that it provides Appropriate Safeguards for Restricted Transfers when it is entered into as a legally binding contract.

This Addendum is dated 1 January 2026 and supplements the EU Standard Contractual Clauses (as defined in Table 2) entered into between the Parties for Restricted Transfers of personal data from the United Kingdom under the UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

Mandatory Clauses

Part 2: Mandatory Clauses of the Approved Addendum, being the template Addendum B.1.0 issued by the ICO and laid before Parliament in accordance with section 119A of the Data Protection Act 2018 on 2 February 2022, as revised under section 18, apply to and are incorporated into this Addendum.

The following Mandatory Clauses (Clauses 1 through 22 of the Addendum B.1.0) are incorporated by reference:

Clause 1 (Definitions): terms used in this Addendum have the meanings given in the Approved EU SCCs, the UK GDPR, and the Data Protection Act 2018.

Clause 2 (Legal Effect): this Addendum supplements and forms part of the Approved EU SCCs. In the event of conflict between this Addendum and the Approved EU SCCs, this Addendum prevails.

Clause 3 (Hierarchy): in the event of conflict between this Addendum and the relevant legislation (UK GDPR / Data Protection Act 2018), the legislation prevails.

Clause 4 (Incorporation of the Approved EU SCCs): the Approved EU SCCs are incorporated into and form part of this Addendum, amended to the extent necessary for the Restricted Transfer from the United Kingdom.

Clause 5 (Optional Clauses): no optional clauses are selected beyond those already selected in Schedule 1.

Clauses 6 through 18 set out the operational obligations of the Parties for Restricted Transfers, including: obligations to notify the other Party of changes affecting compliance; obligations on the Importer when receiving access requests from public authorities; obligations to suspend transfers and terminate this Addendum on non-compliance; and the process for updates where the ICO issues a revised Approved Addendum.

Clause 19 — Termination of this Addendum

Either Party may end this Addendum by giving one month's written notice to the other if the ICO issues a revised Approved Addendum under section 119A of the Data Protection Act 2018. The Parties shall cooperate in good faith to execute a replacement addendum within that notice period.

Clause 20 — Changes to this Addendum

Where the ICO issues a revised Approved Addendum and neither Party has exercised its right to end this Addendum, this Addendum will be automatically superseded by the revised Approved Addendum.

Clause 21 — Dispute Resolution

Disputes arising under this Addendum shall be resolved in accordance with the dispute-resolution clause in the Approved EU SCCs. The governing law of this Addendum is the law of England and Wales.

Clause 22 — Entire Agreement

This Addendum, together with the Approved EU SCCs and the Master Services Agreement, constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties with respect to Restricted Transfers from the United Kingdom to the United States.

References in this Addendum to the UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018 are to those enactments as they have effect in the United Kingdom from time to time.
